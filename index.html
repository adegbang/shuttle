    Welcome to Shuttlefy

    Shuttlefy is a vehicle monitoring platform for shuttles on the University of Ghana campus.

    Shuttlefy Services

    Shuttlefy provides real-time tracking of shuttles on campus as well as providing a payment platform for students to pay their fares. Shuttlefy has a friendly user interface providing a wonderful user experience.
